    About

    St. Jude P/S KAKOOGE is a Catholic founded school found in Luweero Diocese. The school is under the administration of the Brothers of Christian Instruction commonly known as KISUBI BROTHERS. The current head teacher is Bro. Kyamanywa Stephen

    Values of Our School

    Below are the school mission, vision and motto.

    • SCHOOL MISSION: To provide a wholesome high standard of Education that glorifies God and benefits humanity.
    • SCHOOL VISION: To produce resourceful citizens that excel morally and academically.
    • SCHOOL MOTTO: Strive For Excellence.

    The above listed are the core values followed by our schools to deliver quality education to our learners.

    Services

    Below are some of the services provided at St. Jude Primary School.

    Education

    We provide education services all all pupils from Nursery to Primary Seven.

    Religion

    We also provide religious services to our pupils which helps them to group with God fearing hearts and become responsible citizens in the future.

    Sports

    Our school participates in sports activities which help pupils to discover and develop their talents.

    Educational Trips

    At St. Jude Primary School, we organise trips and tours for our learners to help them relate what is being taught in class with the real world.

    Counselling

    We provide guidance and counselling to our pupils to prevent them from making bad decisions and to become useful and productive citizens to the nation in the future.

    Prayers

    Since our school has religious leaders, we conduct prayers within the school premises.
